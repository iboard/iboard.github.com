Elixir in Production: A Retrospective

Posted on Jan 30, 2023
#TAGS
  • archived

Having run Elixir and Phoenix applications in production for several years now, I can confidently say it was the right choice. The stability of the BEAM, the performance of the applications, and the developer experience are all top-notch. The ecosystem has matured, and the community is one of the most welcoming and helpful I’ve ever been a part of. If you’re building for the long term, Elixir and Phoenix are a combination that’s hard to beat.
